AmuletoBet

A new high-end gaming experience.

Goals

Create a brand aimed at aspirational Brazilians.

Deliverable

User interface design.

↓

The challenge

With Hero Gaming expanding into Latin America, we wanted a product launch for the new market. A team was built with product owners, stakeholders and developers. I worked with one other designer.

Finding an angle

We first tapped into the strong Brazilian culture of luck and superstition. We had a site name born from the amulet that many Brazilians carry day-to-day. Using this, we tried various designs using symbols of good luck and the idea that using our site would bring you good luck. This had a flaw - there’s two sides to luck.

Experimenting with visuals and logos, we found the idea wasn’t strong enough and got lost among our competitors. We then developed a new direction.

Choosing the luxury experience

How could we replicate the luxury casino experience online? From our competitor research, we knew that most online casinos used fun and colourful visuals to catch the eye. We were trying something unique in creating a high-end experience.

“Imagine James Bond in Monte Carlo instead of a weekend in Las Vegas.”

We developed visuals inspired by high-end brands. This gave a feeling of calmness and restraint, which is useful in a casino environment. Multiple iterations were produced, with improvements made on landing page layout, logos, imagery and UX writing.

Solution

After six months, we had a visual design that gave a feeling of luxury through a clean logo, minimalist visuals and aspirational lifestyle imagery.
